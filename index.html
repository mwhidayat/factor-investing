PORTFOLIO DESCRIPTION

Momentum40 is a market-neutral portfolio that combines six orthogonal factors:

  • Enhanced Momentum
  • Enhanced Carry
  • Retail Flow
  • Margin Risk
  • Altair
  • Mean Reversion

The asset universe consists of the most liquid and actively traded assets, identified on a rolling basis — various techniques employed to keep it both stable and relevant, as well as survivorship-bias free.

To balance each asset's risk contribution, positions are scaled according to the inverse of their rolling volatility. The portfolio is rebalanced daily, with hourly updates provided, 5 minutes past the hour (UTC).

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
What is the construction methodology? How do I replicate it?+

The portfolio is constructed by applying cross-sectional binning — quantiles. This ensures weights are calculated in proportion to the strength of each factor. Assets are then weighted according to the inverse of their rolling volatility, to mitigate the impact of widely different volatilities of digital assets. We provide a replication notebook in the Resources section that will produce a very similar portfolio to what's shown.

Is the raw factor data available?+

Yes, we serve the raw factor data via the portfolio/factors endpoint. Please see the API Docs for more information. Subscribers receive full access to factor scores, quantile assignments, and IC data for all assets in the universe.

What is smoothing? Why is it applied to some factors?+

Some cross-sectional factors have very high turnover and, despite being highly predictive, the alpha does not survive transaction costs. Smoothing (applying a simple moving average) is a way to reduce turnover and the impact of transaction costs. By default, Momentum40 runs with no smoothing applied.

What is the universe? Is it survivorship-bias free?+

The universe (Top 40 market cap digital assets) is the most liquid and actively traded assets, identified on a rolling basis using volume, open interest, and volatility filters — employed to keep it both stable and relevant. The universe is survivorship-bias free.

What is the rebalancing frequency?+

The portfolio is rebalanced daily, with hourly updates 5 minutes past the hour (UTC), and available point-in-time. This means you can always retrieve what the portfolio looked like at any prior timestamp.

What are the transaction cost / slippage assumptions?+

There are 0.05% transaction costs applied on each position adjustment. Slippage and spread are not considered in the backtest shown on this site. We encourage using independent backtesting infrastructure to validate performance.

When were the factors developed? Is there an out-of-sample period?+

Our research cutoff is 2024-01-01 — we consider any data from that date onwards as out-of-sample. Many factors have been traded live in some form from 2025-01-01.

Should I pick an individual factor and start trading it?+

We recommend using a portfolio of factors to maximize risk-adjusted returns. Alpha from individual factors is simply not consistent enough, and even a simple arithmetic average of 3–5 relatively orthogonal factors will produce superior results.
