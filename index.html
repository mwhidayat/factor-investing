PORTFOLIO DESCRIPTION

Momentum40 is a market-neutral crypto portfolio that combines five orthogonal cross-sectional factors. To balance each asset's risk contribution, positions are scaled according to the inverse of their rolling volatility.

The asset universe consists of the 40 most liquid and actively traded assets, identified on a rolling basis using volume, open interest, and volatility filters — survivorship-bias free.

  • Cross-Sectional Momentum
  • Mean Reversion
  • Enhanced Carry
  • Retail Flow Signal
  • Margin Risk Factor
